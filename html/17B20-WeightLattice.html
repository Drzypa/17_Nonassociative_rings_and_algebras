weight lattice


The weight lattice ΛW of a root system R⊂E is the lattice

ΛW={e∈E|(e,α)(α,α)∈ℤ⁢ for all ⁢r∈R}.

Weights which lie in the weight lattice are called . If R⊂𝔥 is the root system of a semi-simple Lie algebra 𝔤 with Cartan subalgebra 𝔥, then ΛW is exactly the set of weights appearing in finite dimensional representations of 𝔤.
